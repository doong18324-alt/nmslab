PT, PhD · Principal Investigator

Assistant Professor and Chair, Department of Senior Exercise Prescription, Gwangju Health University. His work joins three things that are usually kept apart: how the older cortex behaves under physical load, why pain persists after tissue has healed, and what it takes for a prediction model to survive contact with a real community.
